Financial Modeling of Companies > Income Statement

This is going to be our first dive into making the model. The income statement is a glance at how the business is fundamentally ran. On a basic level, it shows us how much money comes in from their product, how much they spend delivering their product and the various gains and expenses from things like marketing or legal. So let's go ahead and go to our main model tab in our workbook and find the income statement on SEC.gov.
