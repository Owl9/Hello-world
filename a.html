Financial Modeling of Companies

So what exactly are we trying to accomplish by making a model? Financial modeling is a pretty routine activity in the career path and world of Corporate Finance. Its goal is to look at a business and all of it's financial activities, forecast where the enterprise may be heading and finally use the insight gained to make more educated decisions related to investing, merging, acquiring, ect. I personally believe modeling is great first tool to learn if you are interested in corporate finance. The hands-on interaction with companies encourages you to teach yourself how different components of a business function, rather than just reading through a list of terms and definitions. My hope for these lessons is that you will be excited and motivated to not only learn these concepts, but also actively ask yourself questions about how to further understand the financial world.
